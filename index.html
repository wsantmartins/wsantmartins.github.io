About

I am a theoretical physicist working on quantum systems far from equilibrium, open quantum dynamics, and the physics of Rydberg atoms and ions. My research combines analytical methods and numerical modelling in AMO physics and quantum optics. I completed my Ph.D. at the University of Tübingen.
